FRACTAL LOVE

AN INTERACTIVE VIDEO-FEEDBACK INSTALLATION

This interactive installation combines video feedback loops, fractal generation, and real-time visitor engagement. Visitors become part of an ever-evolving visual tapestry, with their movements captured and reintegrated into the fractal patterns projected on the gallery walls.
