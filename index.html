FRACTAL LOVE

AN INTERACTIVE VIDEO-FEEDBACK INSTALLATION

FRACTAL LOVE is an interactive light art installation based on video-feedback. A camera observes its own output, creating a feedback loop that results in beautiful, dynamic, geometric patterns. FRACTAL LOVE is highly interactive. Visitors can step between the camera and projection to become part of the fractal in a sort of psychedelic shadow-play. A 3-axis robot moves the camera to alter the appearing images. The patterns appearing in FRACTAL LOVE are not pre-programmed or algorithmically created. They arise entirely through the feedback loop, and depend on camera angle, light conditions and interaction.
